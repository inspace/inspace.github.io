Matt Calder

mattcalder at meta
m.calder at columbia

Seattle, WA

I am an engineer at Meta and adjunct research scientist at the Columbia University Data Science Institute. I received my Ph.D. in computer science from the University of Southern California.

My research primarily focuses on Internet measurement and data-driven network systems on the Internet such as content delivery and traffic engineering. Before Meta, I worked on the Microsoft Azure CDN and then did a two year stint in backbone capacity planning.
